About Me

Welcome to my portfolio! I am a dynamic and results-oriented Data/Business Analyst with a passion for transforming raw data into actionable insights. With over 2 years of experience in requirement analysis, data modeling, and software design, I thrive in fast-paced environments where I can leverage my analytical skills to drive strategic business decisions.

My journey in data analysis began during my academic pursuits at the University of Kwazulu Natal, where I earned a BSc in Applied Mathematics & Statistics. I further honed my skills through practical experience at Mindworx Academy, where I attained an NQF L5 in Business Analysis.

Throughout my career, I have collaborated with cross-functional teams to deliver impactful projects, ranging from database management with SQL and SAP to reporting using MS PowerBI. Proficient in Python, R, Excel, and SAS, I am adept at extracting, cleaning, and visualizing data to uncover valuable insights that drive business performance.

I am committed to continuous learning and staying updated with the latest advancements in data analytics to remain at the forefront of the industry. Passionate about making a meaningful impact, I am excited about the opportunity to contribute my expertise to your organization's success.
